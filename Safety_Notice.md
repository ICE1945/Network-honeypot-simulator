# Safety & Ethical Usage

This project is designed for controlled cybersecurity research and educational use only.

---

## Permitted Use Cases

- Academic learning and demonstrations  
- Cybersecurity research and experimentation  
- Controlled lab environments  
- Blue-team training simulations  

---

## Prohibited Usage

- Deployment on public or unauthorized networks  
- Monitoring users without explicit consent  
- Any malicious or illegal activity  

---

## Responsibility Disclaimer

This project simulates attacker interaction and must be used responsibly.  
The author assumes no liability for misuse or unauthorized deployment.

Users are expected to comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and ethical standards.
